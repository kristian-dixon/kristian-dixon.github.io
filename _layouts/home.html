About

Hi I’m Kris, I make lots of technical stuff both as a hobby and professionally and I wanted somewhere that I could share the things I’ve worked on.


I have a MSc in Computer Science from the University of Hull and I currently work as a Lead Game Developer on a project called Stage at VISR Dynamics.

My core area of interest is in graphics programming, I love everything about it to the point that I spend most of my weekends trying to implement things that I've either read about or seen in a game during the week. This is likely what a lot of this blog will cover, however I can get dragged into other research rabbitholes quite easily.
I also am really interested in computer vision and photogrammetry. I quite like how they're almost the inverse of a lot of graphics programming stuff in that you usually are taking a 2D image and translating it back into 3D.


Outside of programming I also love Hull City AFC. I quite enjoy obsessing over the data side of things and trying to figure out what we need to get promoted. I also enjoy playing it as a sport, I find it quite useful for clearing out my brain.
